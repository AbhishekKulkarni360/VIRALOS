# VIRALOS Features Guide

## Core Features

### 1. AI-Powered Chat Interface

**What it does:**
- Real-time conversation with AI creator strategist
- Multiple AI modes for different strategies
- Persistent conversation memory
- Context-aware responses

**Supported Modes:**
- Strategist: Content planning & brand positioning
- Analyzer: Content improvement & optimization
- Trend Expert: Viral patterns & opportunities
- Accountability Coach: Execution tracking & discipline
- Monetization Guide: Revenue strategies & scaling
- Psychology Expert: Audience understanding & connection

---

### 2. Content Scoring Engine

**Metrics:**
- Viral Score (0-100) - Overall viral potential
- Hook Strength - Opening quality & curiosity gap
- Retention Score - Viewer retention probability
- Emotional Trigger - Psychology & connection
- CTA Effectiveness - Call-to-action strength
- Relatability Score - Audience connection level

**How to use:**
1. Go to Analytics Dashboard
2. Click "Score Content"
3. Enter content details
4. Get instant feedback
5. Implement recommendations

---

### 3. Analytics Dashboard

**Dashboard Elements:**
- Growth chart (audience growth over time)
- Performance chart (content scores)
- Engagement metrics (views, likes, comments)
- Top performing content
- Trend analysis & insights

**Features:**
- Real-time metrics visualization
- Filter by date range
- Compare content performance
- Export data for analysis

---

### 4. Content Calendar

**What it does:**
- Plan content schedule
- Track posting dates
- Monitor consistency
- Organize by platform

**How to use:**
1. Open Content Calendar
2. Click "Add Content"
3. Set date & platform
4. Add content details
5. Save to calendar

---

### 5. Conversation Memory System

**What it does:**
- Remembers all conversation history
- Learns user preferences & style
- Provides personalized advice
- Tracks progress over time

**Benefits:**
- AI improves recommendations over time
- Full history accessible
- Context-aware responses
- Personalized coaching
